Coming From Philadelphia

The nearest airport to Glassboro is the Philadelphia International Airport, located about 24 miles (just under 40 kilometers) from Glassboro. Guests are encouraged to use Rapid Rover to get to Glassboro. A typical shared service fare from the airport is just under $45 for a single adult. Please click here to get quotes and book a ride.

Coming From Newark

The Newark Airport is 94 miles from Glassboro (150 kilometers). Those flying into this airport will need to rent a car and drive to Glassboro or get to Philadelphia via rail service, and then take a bus to Glassboro. Please see below for rail and bus service links.

  • Amtrack

    • Amtrak, the national rail service of the U.S.A., is the primary provider of train travel. The Northeast Corridor is the most heavily developed section of the Amtrak rail network.

  • NJ Transit

    • NJ Transit allows one to go from nearly any point in New Jersey to any other one, including connections to New York City and Philadelphia. NJ Transit is not generally a very quick way to get from place to place, but it will get you there.
